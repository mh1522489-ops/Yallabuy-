Pros & Cons

Before you decide, get a full picture of the strengths and weaknesses.

✅ Pros

  • Excellent battery life — up to 14 days.
  • Sharp AMOLED display with vibrant colors.
  • Competitive price compared to big-name rivals.
  • High accuracy on health metrics (HR / SpO₂ / sleep).
  • Clean companion app, works on iOS & Android.
  • 5ATM water resistance for swimming.

⚠️ Cons

  • Limited third-party app ecosystem.
  • NFC payments not available in all regions.
  • Voice assistant is less smart than Siri / Google.
  • Replacement bands are less varied than Apple Watch.
  • No cellular (eSIM) calling support.
