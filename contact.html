Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How long does delivery take?
Delivery typically takes 1-3 business days within Cairo and 3-5 business days for other governorates. Express delivery options are available at checkout for urgent orders.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 7-day return policy. Items must be in their original condition with all tags attached. Simply contact our support team to initiate the return process.
Is Cash on Delivery available?
Yes! Cash on Delivery (COD) is available across all of Egypt. You can also pay securely online using credit/debit cards or digital wallets.
How can I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you will receive an SMS with a tracking number. You can also track your order in real-time through your account dashboard on our website.
