Frequently Asked Questions

Based on real user feedback, the 3D adjustable armrests on some models cannot be locked in place. This causes constant movement and frustration when you shift or bump them. If you are a fidgety user, you should seriously consider this issue before purchasing. I noticed this myself on the SIHOO M57 — it is annoying if you rest your arms heavily.

Durability varies significantly. One user reported the bottom mesh ripped out after less than a year of use. While comfortable for long periods, some chairs are better suited for occasional or weekend use rather than heavy daily use. I recommend the Herman Miller Aeron or GABRYLLY if you need something that will last.

A user who could not get comfortable returned their chair and purchased the GABRYLLY Ergonomic Office Mesh Chair instead. They found it sturdier, better designed, and similarly priced. They recommend the GABRYLLY over the original chair. I tested it myself and agree — it is the best alternative in this price range.

One user received a chair with a missing headrest part. The seller responded quickly to emails for resolution. The chair still functioned well with great lumbar support even without the headrest. Most sellers on Amazon are responsive, but always check the return policy before buying.

There have been reports of users receiving products wrapped from a previous owner with scratches from prior assembly. The plastic construction can feel cheap and lack sturdiness. As one buyer noted, "you get what you pay for." I recommend buying directly from the official seller or Amazon directly to minimize this risk.

Sizing can be an issue. One taller user called it one of the most uncomfortable chairs they have owned. The sides dig into the legs, and the back is not broad enough for people with longer legs, making it distracting and unusable. If you are over 6 feet tall, I strongly recommend the GABRYLLY or Herman Miller Aeron instead of budget compact models.

Build quality concerns exist across the budget range. One user reported the seat frame came apart after short use, making the chair unstable and unusable. They were disappointed with the build quality for the price and do not recommend it for reliability. This is why I rank the Herman Miller Aeron and GABRYLLY higher — their frames feel genuinely solid.
