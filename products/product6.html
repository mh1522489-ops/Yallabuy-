As the laser passes over the floor, it transforms invisible dust into clearly visible glowing particles, allowing you to see what you have been missing all along. The result? Cleaning that is up to 2x more precise and thorough compared to traditional vacuums.

Key Features

Advanced technologies that make cleaning a different experience

01

Intelligent Piezo Sensor

The sensor counts the size and number of particles being captured 15,000 times per second. It sends data to the motor to automatically adjust suction power based on dirt levels — saving battery and ensuring maximum efficiency.

02

Interactive LCD Screen

The screen displays real-time particle data with a graph showing the size of captured particles (>10μm, >60μm, >180μm, >500μm). It also shows remaining battery life and maintenance alerts.

03

Digital Motorbar™ Head

The intelligent cleaning head automatically adjusts brush resistance based on floor type. It includes anti-tangle hair removal technology with 56 polycarbonate blades preventing 99% of hair tangles.

04

Whole-Machine HEPA Filtration

A fully sealed system with a HEPA filter that traps 99.99% of microscopic particles as small as 0.3 microns. Ideal for allergy and asthma sufferers, ensuring no particles escape back into the air.

05

Detachable Battery

A high-efficiency lithium-ion battery that lasts up to 68 minutes in Eco mode. It can be detached with a single click to recharge or replaced with a spare battery to extend cleaning time.

06

Ergonomic Design

The hand unit weighs just 3.1 kg with optimal weight distribution. Comfortable for extended use without fatigue. Easily converts to a handheld vacuum for cleaning cars, sofas, and stairs.
