Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you buy

Is the built-in grinder adjustable for different coffee types?

Yes! The integrated conical burr grinder features 16 precise grind settings, from ultra-fine for espresso to coarse for French press. The dose control grinding function lets you dial in the exact amount of coffee for each brew, ensuring consistent results every time.

Is this machine beginner-friendly?

Absolutely. The Barista Express is designed with guided controls and clear indicators that walk you through each step — from grinding to tamping to extraction. Most beginners pull their first great shot within a day, and the included manual plus online tutorials make learning effortless.

What kind of warranty does it come with?

The Breville Barista Express comes with a 2-year limited product warranty that covers defects in materials and workmanship. Amazon also offers a 30-day hassle-free return policy, so you can try it risk-free.

How do I clean and maintain the machine?

Daily maintenance takes just a few minutes: rinse the portafilter, wipe the steam wand, and empty the drip tray. The machine includes a cleaning alert system and comes with cleaning tools and tablets. A full descale cycle is only needed every few months and takes under 30 minutes.

What cup sizes are compatible with the machine?

The adjustable drip tray accommodates cups and mugs up to 3.75 inches tall for single cups, and can be removed entirely to fit travel mugs up to 5.5 inches. This means everything from demitasse espresso cups to your favorite oversized morning mug will fit perfectly.

Can I make lattes and cappuccinos with this machine?

Yes! The powerful steam wand creates barista-quality microfoam perfect for lattes, cappuccinos, flat whites, and more. The included stainless steel milk jug is specifically designed for optimal frothing. You'll be creating latte art in no time.
