Key Features

What Makes It Special

Standout features that set the Anker 737 apart from the competition

Smart Digital Display

Real-time monitoring of output/input power, battery percentage, estimated time to full charge/discharge, internal temperature, and lifetime usage stats including charge cycles and battery health.

140W Two-Way Fast Charging

Both USB-C ports support full 140W output with Power Delivery 3.1. The power bank itself recharges at 140W too, going from 0% to 100% in just about 1 hour with a compatible charger.

Laptop-Grade Power

Capable of charging a 16-inch MacBook Pro at full speed via MagSafe. Also supports Dell, HP, ASUS, and other USB-C laptops. Charges iPhone 13 almost 5 times, iPad Pro 12.9" 1.3 times.

GaNPrime Technology

Anker's Gallium Nitride technology delivers higher power conversion with lower temperatures, allowing a more compact design. ActiveShield 2.0 monitors temperature 3 million times per day for safety.

PPS Support for Samsung

Supports PPS protocol enabling 45W Super Fast Charging 2.0 on Samsung Galaxy S22 Ultra, S23 Ultra, and other PPS-compatible devices. Many competitors lack this feature.

Pass-Through Charging

Charge the power bank while simultaneously charging your devices. Perfect for using as a desktop charging hub or when you have limited wall outlets while traveling.

Cons
